Our Future Plannings:

Service we want to provide

Selling the Nakshi Kathas in low rates :

Bangladesh, eventhough a developing country, has still a majority of people below the poverty line. We have to keep in mind that they are also deserving of our culture and its benefits.

Donating the Nakshi Kathas to the people in need :

Now, even after the lowering of costs, majority of our people will not be able to afford one. So, occasionally our team will send out donations to people in need.








Selling the Nakshi Kathas to people with "Modern" lifestyles :

The rich people of Bangladesh are seen to preffer western products over our traditions. Our team has concluded that this has happened due to lack of professional presentation of the products. We plan on selling these on that standard.



Introducing Nakshi Kathas to foreigners :

Bangladesh is renowned worldwide as a tourist place with rich diversity in cluture and history. So, naturally, many tourists come by now and then. If we are able to catch their interests in our products, we will surely be successful.



Selling other handicrafts other than Nakshi Kathas :

Nakshi Katha is one of the most renowned handicrafts of the artisans of Bangladesh. Again, potters make dolls, earthen pots etc. By this, we can expand our domain in business.
